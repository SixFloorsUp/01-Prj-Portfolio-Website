About Me

I'm an undergraduate student at ASU Online, currently based in Seattle and originally from Hawaii. My interest in computers began at 12 when I joined a basic JavaScript coding club with my best friend, sparking my new found passion and interest for technology. Outside of tech, I enjoy swimming, surfing, video games, and anything that keeps me connected to the water.

My interest in UX/UI design grew during my internship at Starbucks Support Center, where I independently created a UX project in my free time. I designed a fully functional prototype using Figma that addressed a real usability problem with my own proposed solutions, conducted usability testing with peers from diverse work backgrounds, and synthesized the results into a findings share-out presented to senior leaders and managers in corporate.
